Attendance Update

Update Employee Attendance

Update the attendance of your employees using this form:

  • Enter the employee's ID The employee ID is unique to each employee
  • Enter the date of the attendance
  • Enter the status of the attendance (Present or Absent)
  • Enter the time in (if present)
  • Enter the time out (if present) The time out have to be after the time in
  • Enter the comment This is optional
