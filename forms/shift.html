  • Shift Name Give the shift a unique name for easy identification.
  • Start Times
  • End Times
  • Frequency
  • Days of the Week Select the days of the week this shift occurs (for weekly repeats).
  • Start Date
  • End Date Define the duration of the recurring shift period.
